I've had a few blogs in my life, each one killed by some combination of embarrassment and neglect. The first was a Wordpress I made when I was fourteen because I thought having a blog made you a writer in the same way having a stethoscope makes you a doctor. The second was a Blogger I grew quite attached to before deleting in a fit of paranoia that someone I knew might actually read it. This is the third, and I have decided not to concern myself with its fate.

I suppose the idea is to write things down before they disappear. I've noticed life has a nasty habit of happening faster than I can process it, and by the time I sit down to reflect on something, three newer things have already taken its place. I don't have a particularly good memory either — I once forgot my own phone number at a doctor's office — so maybe writing is less of an art for me and more of a logistical necessity.

This occupies roughly 30MB of SSD somewhere in Virginia or Oregon, which feels like an adequate amount of space for whatever it is I have to say. If you are somehow reading this, I apologise in advance.
