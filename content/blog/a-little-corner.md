I've been thinking about how much happens in life constantly and how easily it all gets forgotten as new memories take the place of old ones. But some memories are worth making space for.

So I've started this blog. A small catalogue of moments, occupying a quiet corner of the internet roughly 30MB of SSD, 500MB of DDR SDRAM, and 10m vCPU accessible from anywhere.
