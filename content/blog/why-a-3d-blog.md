Most personal sites are resumes pretending to be blogs. I wanted the opposite, a place that feels like a home, where each thing I care about gets its own object in a room.

The bet: meaning lives in *where* and *how* you encounter something, not just *what*. A blog post on a notebook at a sunlit desk is the same words as `/blog/why-a-3d-blog`, but it arrives differently.

Let's see if it holds up.
